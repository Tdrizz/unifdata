## Narration

Your business runs across sticky notes, spreadsheets, and three different apps.
unifdata pulls it into one.

Start your morning with a live brief — follow-ups due, jobs active, revenue outstanding.
Not a tutorial. Not a placeholder. Your actual data, right now.

Every customer. Every job. Every lead in your pipeline. Every payment you're owed.
Add anything in seconds with the quick-add forms built into every page.

Connect Stripe, HubSpot, or Jobber — your existing records sync in automatically.
Then review, confirm, and merge. No overwrites.

It works the way your business works.
unifdata.
